## Overall Impression

The quote viewer is one of the strongest surfaces in the product. Editorial layout, three-tier design token cascade, and expiry urgency system show craft. Weakness is entirely in the decision flow: terminal states are flat, error handling is generic, and zero guidance about what acceptance means.

## What's Working

1. **Editorial layout discipline.** Single-column scroll with consistent section rhythm reads like a designed document. 60vh header, fluid clamp typography, Investment card gradient callback.
2. **Three-tier design token cascade.** Per-quote, company branding, and fallback defaults. Architecturally rare and well-executed.
3. **Expiry urgency system.** Five states with escalating visual treatment. Expired state correctly blocks acceptance.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Terminal states lack emotional resolution and next-steps guidance
After accepting, client sees generic "we'll be in touch shortly." For a financial commitment, this is anticlimactic. No confirmation summary, no next-steps, no contact info.
**Fix**: Add confirmation summary with amount, what-happens-next section, company contact info. Consider subtle success animation.
**Command**: `/impeccable harden`

### [P1] Decision panel inputs lack form wrapping — keyboard and a11y impact
Name input, textarea, checkbox, and submit button not wrapped in a form. Enter key does nothing. Screen readers cannot announce form relationship. Mobile keyboards show "Return" instead of "Submit".
**Fix**: Wrap in `<form onSubmit>`. Bind Enter to submission when valid.
**Command**: `/impeccable harden`

### [P1] Focus indicators removed with no replacement
Inputs use `focus:outline-none` with no custom focus ring. Toggle pill buttons have no aria-pressed or role=tab. WCAG 2.4.7 failure.
**Fix**: Add focus-visible ring. Add role=tablist/tab/aria-selected to toggle pill.
**Command**: `/impeccable audit`

### [P2] Breakdown table forces horizontal scroll on mobile
Table has min-w-[480px] with overflow-x-auto. On 375px phone, horizontal scroll inside vertical document.
**Fix**: Stack line items as cards on mobile or collapse columns.
**Command**: `/impeccable adapt`

### [P2] Hero image loads with no skeleton or transition
Hero image pops in when signed URL resolves. Photos fetched sequentially, not in parallel.
**Fix**: Add skeleton/opacity transition. Fetch URLs with Promise.all.
**Command**: `/impeccable polish`

## Persona Red Flags

**Jordan (First-Timer)**: Types name and presses Enter — nothing happens. Agreement checkbox says "terms above" but hasn't scrolled back. After accepting: zero information about what happens next.

**Casey (Mobile User)**: Breakdown table forces horizontal scroll at 375px. Toggle pill buttons are 28px tall — tight for thumb tapping. Download PDF button may overlap decision panel on short viewports.

**Sam (Accessibility)**: focus:outline-none removes focus visibility (WCAG 2.4.7). Toggle buttons lack aria-pressed/role=tab. Faint section labels at ~2.8:1 contrast fail AA.
